YOU ARE NOT ALONE!

A simp is someone who does way too much for another person while ignoring their own wants and needs. It’s usually used about men/guys who desperately try to get attention from women and, due to lacking social skills, attempt to compensate with money, excessive attention, flattery, or similar behavior. This kind of behavior typically inspires only pity.

HISTORY OF THE WORD

The word “simp” comes from the English simpleton (“a fool, a naive person”) and was first recorded as the shortened form simp around 1903. In the 1980s, it began to be used in hip-hop culture to describe a man who excessively pleases a woman or puts her on a pedestal. Later, around 2019, the word started appearing widely across the internet.

HOW TO USE

It's usually used in a negative context like: “OMG! He is such a simp, so pathetic!” or in an ironic way like: “I am such a simp for her!”, BUT it's important to see the difference between person with sympathy and a simp
